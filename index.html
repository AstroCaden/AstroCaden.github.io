IFJ PAN Internship – Kraków, Poland

During my 2024 research internship at the Institute of Nuclear Physics (IFJ PAN), I studied the internal structure of hybrid neutron stars within the Einstein Telescope detection range. I implemented and refined a Tolman–Oppenheimer–Volkoff solver, converting it from C++ to Python to analyse over 100 candidate equations of state, producing mass–radius relations and tidal deformability plots.
