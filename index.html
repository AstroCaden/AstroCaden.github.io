Research

IFJ PAN – Kraków, Poland

Summer 2024 research internship at the Institute of Nuclear Physics (IFJ PAN). My work focused on studying the internal structure of hybrid neutron stars within the Einstein Telescope detection range. I implemented and refined a Tolman–Oppenheimer–Volkoff solver, converting it from C++ to Python to analyse over 100 candidate equations of state, producing mass–radius relations, pressure–density profiles, and tidal deformabilities.

Download IFJ PAN Internship Report

Teide Observatory – Tenerife, Spain

Conducted observational and presentation-based research at the Teide Observatory in Tenerife. This project involved developing presentation material and data analysis for small-telescope observations, focusing on observational methods and outreach.
